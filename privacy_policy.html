RetroTracker Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Summary: RetroTracker does not collect, store, or share your personal data on any external servers. Your credentials are stored locally on your device only.

1. Information We Collect

RetroTracker collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • RetroAchievements Username: Used to fetch your profile and achievement data
  • RetroAchievements API Key: Used to authenticate with the RetroAchievements.org API
  • App Preferences: Your theme settings, notification preferences, and favorite games

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Connect directly to RetroAchievements.org to fetch your profile, games, and achievements
  • Display your progress within the app
  • Save your preferences for a personalized experience

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Your API key is encrypted using your device's secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain)
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not operate any backend servers
  • We never transmit your credentials to any server other than RetroAchievements.org
  • All network communication uses HTTPS encryption

4. Third-Party Services

RetroTracker uses the following third-party services:

  • RetroAchievements.org API: To fetch your gaming data. Their privacy policy applies to data stored on their servers.
  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements (free version only). Google may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing: To process premium purchases. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data stays on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

RetroTracker does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences who have RetroAchievements accounts.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings
  • Log out at any time, which removes your stored credentials
  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated effective date.

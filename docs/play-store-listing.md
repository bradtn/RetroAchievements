## Full Description

**RetroTrack - Your RetroAchievements Companion**

Track your retro gaming journey with RetroTrack, the ultimate companion app for RetroAchievements.org. Monitor your progress, maintain your streaks, and never miss an achievement again.

**Dashboard & Stats**
- View your complete profile with points, rank, and achievements
- Track recently played games and earned achievements
- See your gaming activity calendar at a glance

**Streak Tracking**
- Monitor your daily achievement streak
- Set evening reminders to protect your streak
- Celebrate milestones at 7, 30, 100+ days

**Game Explorer**
- Browse games by console
- Search the entire RetroAchievements library
- View detailed achievement lists and progress
- Track game completion percentage

**Achievement of the Week**
- Stay updated on weekly community challenges
- See who's unlocking achievements in real-time
- Tap usernames to view player profiles

**Social Features**
- Compare stats with friends
- View other players' profiles and progress
- Share achievement cards to social media

**Home Screen Widgets**
- Recent achievements widget with auto-cycling
- Streak counter widget
- Achievement of the Week widget
- Game progress tracker widget

**Smart Notifications**
- Daily streak reminders at your preferred time
- Achievement of the Week alerts
- Milestone celebrations

**Customization**
- Light, Dark, and AMOLED themes
- Multiple accent color options
- Clean, modern Material 3 design

**Note:** This app requires a RetroAchievements.org account and API key. RetroTrack is a third-party companion app and is not affiliated with RetroAchievements.org.

Start tracking your retro gaming achievements today!

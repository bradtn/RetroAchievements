# Privacy Policy for RetroTrack

**Last updated: [DATE]**

## Overview

RetroTrack is a companion app for RetroAchievements.org. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **RetroAchievements Username**: Used to fetch your profile and gaming data from RetroAchievements.org
- **API Key**: Stored securely on your device to authenticate with RetroAchievements.org

### Data from RetroAchievements.org
We retrieve the following data from RetroAchievements.org using their public API:
- Profile information (username, points, rank)
- Game progress and achievements
- Recent activity

This data is fetched directly from RetroAchievements.org and displayed in the app.

## Data Storage

- Your API key is stored securely on your device using encrypted storage
- App preferences are stored locally on your device
- We do not store your data on external servers

## Third-Party Services

### RetroAchievements.org
This app connects to RetroAchievements.org to fetch your gaming data. Please refer to their privacy policy at https://retroachievements.org/legal.php

### Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information for ad personalization. See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Google Play Services
Required for in-app purchases (premium features).

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by the third-party services listed above.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
